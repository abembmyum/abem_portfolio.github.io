Overview

Proposed a framework for detecting depression and identifying levels of depression using a time-series dataset. Applied Ant colony Optimization algorithm for feature selection and used classifiers such as Random Forests, XGBoost, Support Vector Machine, DNN and 1D-CNN. Achieved F1 score of 89.9% compared to 72% of the previous research.
